COLLABORATE. COMPLIMENT.

Collaborate with artists, at your own pace.

Collaborate

No art piece is alone in Spart. At any point an artist can connect their own work with yours for an amplified viewing and listening experience.

Express

Spart is a place where you can express your interpretation of art through music, visuals, and writing. At the same time you can view an endless network of how other artists interpret artwork.

Engage

The pattern of posting art and walking away without more interaction is over. At Spart we aim to create a platform where artists want to stay, browse, and interact with artists in more creative ways than before.

Interact

Spart is just as much a community as it is a platform. We want to make your voice, concerns, and requests heard for the best experience possible. If there is something you would like to see in the platform you can mention it to us and also discuss it with other artists. We will do our best to make it happen.

A low pressure environment where you can collaborate with and inspire other artists.

Who are we?

Well, we are actually just one person at the moment but more than that we are a community. This platform would be nothing without the people that interact with it.
