Collaborate

No piece of artwork is alone in Spart. At any point an artist can connect their own work with yours for an amplified viewing, writing, and or listening experience.

Express

Spart is a place where you can express your interpretation of art through music, visuals, and writing. At the same time you can view an endless network of how other artists interpret artwork.

Engage

No more just posting, commenting, and liking. At Spart we aim to create a platform where artists want to stay, browse, and interact with artists in creative ways.

Interact

Spart is just as much a community as it is a platform. We want to make your voice, concerns, and requests heard for the best experience possible. If there is something you would like to see in the platform you can mention it to us. We will do our best to make it happen.

A low pressure environment where you can collaborate with and inspire other artists.

Who are we?
